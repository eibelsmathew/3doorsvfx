3 DOORS VFX

Section 1

We visualize your story at every stage of production — from storyboarding to on-set support to postproduction. We work closely with you to explore the narrative, design shots, and plan effective technical solutions.

Section 2

Even before your project is greenlit, we help you bring your concept to life with a tailored previsualization trailer — giving you a clear vision of your story early in the process

Section 3

We begin the visualization process with detailed storyboards that capture the key beats of each scene. These are then edited into animatics by our editorial team, providing a clear and reviewable blueprint for the sequence."

Section 4

Through 3D visualization, you can explore every movement and camera angle in detail. Previs acts as your creative testing ground — helping you refine ideas and plan confidently before the shoot.

Section 5

Our workflow stores all technical information (lens type, camera distance, size of a greenscreen, speed of a vehicle) and produces blueprints for physical production.

Section 6

After production, we seamlessly integrate our previsualization elements with the live-action footage to create a quick yet comprehensive preview of the film—allowing directors and producers to visualize the final look before full-scale VFX implementation
